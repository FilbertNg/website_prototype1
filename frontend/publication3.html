CRISPR-Cas9 Gene Editing for Drought Resistance in Local Rice Varieties

Journal: Nature Communications

Authors: Chris Kim, Dana White, Eric Johnson, Fiona Lee, et al.

Impact Factor & Classification: 15.7 (WoS Q1, Scopus-Indexed)

Access DOI

Executive Summary

This research details the use of CRISPR-Cas9 to enhance drought resistance in local rice varieties, achieving a 50% increase in survival rates under water-scarce conditions. The findings offer a promising solution for climate adaptation in agriculture.
