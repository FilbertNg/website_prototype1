Sustainable Water Management Using AI in Arid Regions

Journal: Water Resources Journal

Authors: Emily Chen, Mark Taylor, Sarah Johnson, et al.

Impact Factor & Classification: 7.8 (WoS Q2, Scopus-Indexed)

Access DOI

Executive Summary

This study presents an AI-based water management system for arid regions, reducing water waste by 35% through predictive analytics and smart irrigation.
