A Novel Approach to Bioconversion of Sugarcane Bagasse into Bioethanol

Journal: Proceedings of the International Conference on Renewable Energy

Authors: Alice Johnson, Bob Lee, Carol Taylor, David Miller, et al.

Impact Factor & Classification: N/A (Conference Paper, Non-Indexed)

Access DOI

Executive Summary

This study introduces an innovative bioconversion process for converting sugarcane bagasse into bioethanol, reducing agricultural waste and supporting a zero-waste economy. The method achieved a 40% conversion efficiency in lab-scale trials.
